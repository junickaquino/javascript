
LinkedList
    - Good at adding and deleting new nodes/items
    - Not good at retrieval or searching

Array
    - Good at retrieving items
    - Not good at adding items

Hash Table
    - or dictionary
    - Good at retrieving and adding
    - But collision could be a problem

Stack
    - Last in first out
    - Push and pop

Queue
    - First in First out
    - Enqueue
    - dequeue

Graphs
    - Similar to linkedList, but nodes are called edges.

Tree
    - parent and children
    - Binary search tree
